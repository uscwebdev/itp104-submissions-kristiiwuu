Breakfast

Eggs Benedict

Ingredients:

  • Egg
  • English muffin
  • Vinegar
  • Water
  • Butter
  • Egg yolk
  • Lime juice
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt & pepper

Procedure:

  1. Toast English muffin
  2. Cook slice of ham/ canadian bacon (optional)

(Egg Poaching)

  1. Fill pot with 3 inches of water and boil; then reduce to a simmer
  2. Add splash of vineger
  3. Crack an egg into a small cup/bowl
  4. Lower egg into simmering water
  5. Cook for 3-5 minutes
  6. Remove with splotted spoon

(Hollandaise Sauce)

  1. Melt butter in saucepan
  2. Beat egg yolks in separate bowl
  3. Add lime juice, heavy whipping cream, salt and pepper
  4. Add small amount of hot butter to egg mixture
  5. Stir well
  6. Repeat process while slowly adding one spoonful of hot butter to egg mixture
  7. Add mixture back to saucepan
  8. Cook for few more seconds

Lunch

Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon
  • Butter
  • Salt, pepper, & garlic salt
  • Lemon
  • Asparagus

Procedure:

(Salmon)

  1. Slice salmon into smaller chunks
  2. Wash salmon and dry
  3. Marinate with salt, pepper, and garlic salt
  4. Cut a slice of lemon and place on top of salmon
  5. Using airfryer or pan on stovetop, cook with butter

(Asparagus)

  1. Chop bottom stem of asparagus and wash
  2. Boil water
  3. Place asparagus in boiling water and let it steam for 5 minutes

Dinner

Pasta

Watch Video: Click here

Ingredients:

  • Penne or any pasta (1 cup)
  • Olive oil (2-3 tbsp)
  • Minced garlic (1 tbsp)
  • Finely chopped onion (1/2 onion)
  • Tomato paste (3 tbsp)
  • Red chilli flakes (1 tsp)
  • Black pepper (1/2 tsp)
  • Red chilli powder (1/4 tsp)
  • Granulated sugar (1/2 tsp)
  • Heavy whipping cream (6-7 tbsp)
  • Salted butter (1/2 tbsp)
  • Mozzarella cheese (2 tbsp)
  • Fresh basil leaves (2-3)

Procedure:

  1. Add 1 tsp of salt in boiling water (Don't forget to save 1/4 cup of pasta water)
  2. Cook pasta for 12-13 minutes
  3. Add consecutively 2-3 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp minced garlic, and 1/2 finely chopped onion to pan
  4. Add 3 tbsp tomato paste and cook on low-medium fire for 2-2.5 minutes
  5. Add 1 tsp red chilli flakes, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and salt per taste; mix in
  6. Add 6-7 tbsp heavy whipping cream; mix
  7. Add 1/2 tbsp butter
  8. Add 1/4 cup of pasta water
  9. Add cooked pasta and mix into sauce
  10. Optional: add mozzarella cheese and top with basil leaves
