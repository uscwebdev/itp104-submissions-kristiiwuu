Topic

My website will be dedicated towards a small real estate brokerage. The website serves to attract potential clients, showcase listings, and provide other useful information, such as contact information and resources.

Audience

My intended audience includes homebuyers, sellers, and people looking for a real estate agent to represent them.

Design & Style

Since the company's logo is red and white, I will incorporate these colors into the website. Overall, I aim to maintain a professional and sleek design.

Tentative Color Pallete:

  • Primary Colors
    • red (#D63646)
    • black (#080707)
    • white (#FFFFFF)
  • Secondary Colors
    • maroon (#BD4F6C)
    • light green (#DDEDAA)
    • yellow (#F0CF65)
    • gray (#B3B3B3)
